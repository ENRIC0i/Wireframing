About Me




Hello! My name is Enrico Abenes, and I am currently an Information Technology student majoring in Network and Security. I was born on July 29, 2000, in the picturesque city of Baguio, Philippines, which has always been home to me. Now, I reside in San Roque Village, a peaceful and close-knit community in the heart of Baguio. I take great pride in my Filipino roots, and being from such a vibrant city has given me a unique perspective on the importance of both local and global connectivity in the world of technology.

From a young age, I’ve been curious about how things work, particularly when it comes to technology. This led me to pursue a degree in IT, where I specialize in network management and cybersecurity. With the rapid growth of digital systems, I’m passionate about learning how to protect data and secure online networks. I find satisfaction in solving complex problems and ensuring that systems function efficiently and securely.

In addition to my studies, I enjoy spending time with family and friends and exploring Baguio’s beautiful surroundings. I also stay up-to-date with the latest trends in technology, always eager to learn more and expand my knowledge. I value meaningful connections with others, especially those who share similar interests in technology.

As I continue my academic journey, I’m excited to build a career in network security. My goal is to contribute to making the digital world a safer place and to grow professionally in this ever-evolving field.
